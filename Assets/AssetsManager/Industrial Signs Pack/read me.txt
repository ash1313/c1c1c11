The "industrial signs pack" contains 35 signs and 3 assets ready to fill up your level.
good for third person and fps games.


All objects are .fbx format files and all the texture maps are .tga files.

This pack contains:
- LODs where needed.
- al the objects contains a specular and a diffuse map.
- Some objects has a normal map.
- Extra uv channel for your lightmaps.
